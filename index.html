About Me

My name is Jarod Reichel, and I am an undergraduate Computer Science Student at James Madison University. I am 18 years old, and my birthday is July 21st. Ever since a young age, I have had a passion for Computers. I have been programming since I was 11 years old, when I picked up a beginner's book on C++ at the public library.

I attended Fauquier High School in Warrenton, Virginia. Having such a strong interest in Computer Science, I took every CS Course offered at my school, which included Computer Math (Visual Basic), AP Computer Science A, and AP Computer Science Principles. I graduated on May 22, 2019, and a few months later began studying at JMU.

At JMU, I am in the Honors College, and as such will complete a Capstone Project before I graduate in 2023. My academic interests within Computer Science include Software Development/Engineering and Computer Systems. I am also interested in the area of Computer Science Education (especially with regards to teaching CS to Middle and High Schoolers) and I am an advocate for a more widely available K12 CS Curriculum.
